About EuroFix

EuroFix Cleaning & Refurbish is an international company founded on years of hands‑on experience in cleaning, facilities, and building environments across London and beyond. We specialize in professional cleaning, maintenance, and refurbishment for residential and commercial properties.

Our founder brings more than six years in hospitality and commercial cleaning, a strong commitment to hygiene and safety, and practical construction knowledge. With certifications in electrical installation Level 2 & 3, the City & Guilds 2382‑22 18th Edition qualification, and Level 3 solar panel training, we understand the technical and safety demands of modern properties. This expertise lets us provide services that go beyond surface cleaning—supporting repairs, installations, and upgrades with professionalism and care.

Mission statement

We exist to deliver excellence in cleaning and refurbishment through skilled work, dependable service, and modern practices. Our mission is to improve living and working environments by combining meticulous attention to detail, health and safety compliance, and respectful customer care—every time, everywhere.
